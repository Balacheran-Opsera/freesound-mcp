Hi! It looks like you have not signed up for a very long time and you have sounds with very old Creative Commons licenses. We need you to pick a new license for your uploads!

For a while our friends at Creative Commons have been telling us that the Sampling+ license used before 2011 in Freesound is not a good license, and especially for sounds. The reasons are all quite technical legal arguments, but we agree with the Creative Commons guys and we are helping to rid the world of this license.

We now only support 3 licenses and would like for you to select one of these for your old uploads before you continue. This might sound dramatic, but it isn't really. The terminology used in these new licenses is much clearer and there are fewer grey areas. It's better for you, for us, and downloaders of your sounds.

Please note: you will be able to change the license of any of your sounds whenever you want on the individual sounds' edit pages.
